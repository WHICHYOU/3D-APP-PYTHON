# Executive Summary - 2D to 3D SBS Conversion Software

**Document Type:** Executive Summary

## Opportunity

The VR/AR/3D display market is experiencing explosive growth, but faces a critical **content gap**. While hardware is rapidly improving (Meta Quest 3, Apple Vision Pro, smart glasses), users have limited native 3D content to enjoy on these devices. Our AI-powered 2D to 3D conversion software solves this problem by instantly transforming billions of existing 2D videos and images into immersive 3D experiences.

### Market Size

- **VR Headset Market:** Expected to reach $28.5B by 2028 (CAGR 31.5%)
- **AR Smart Glasses:** Projected $70B market by 2030
- **3D Display Market:** $180B+ globally
- **Total Addressable Market (TAM):** $280B+ hardware market needing content solutions

---

## Solution

**Professional AI-powered desktop application** that:

- Converts any 2D video or image to 3D Side-by-Side (SBS) format
- Uses state-of-the-art AI depth estimation (MiDaS, Depth-Anything-V2)
- GPU-accelerated processing (30-60 fps for 1080p)
- User-friendly interface with real-time preview
- Supports 4K/8K output

**B2B SDK** for hardware manufacturers:

- Easy integration into VR headsets, smart glasses, 3D displays
- Optimized for edge/mobile chipsets
- Flexible deployment (native, cloud, hybrid)
- White-label option
